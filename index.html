Research Interests

My research explores how neural networks learn useful visual representations and how these internal mechanisms can be interpreted.

Representation Learning

Learning compact, discriminative, and transferable representations for visual recognition, matching, and retrieval.

Mechanism Interpretability

Understanding neuron concepts, internal representations, and task-relevant information in deep neural networks.

Computer Vision

Developing efficient visual descriptors and interpretable methods for image-level and local visual tasks.
